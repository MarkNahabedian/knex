Background

The motor's power comes from an AC adapter that produces 12VDC at 500ma.

The power adapter connects to the motor via a mini power plug 2.1mm in diameter and 9mm long. I don't know the inside pin diameter.

The switch is to be mounted in the center hole of a connector. That hole is 0.25" in diameter. The connectors are 0.25" thick.

Printed Circuit Board

The recommended trace width for 0.5A ia 0.02".

The recommended gap between traces is 0.01".

For low density boards recommended grid snap is 0.05 inch (1.27mm).

ExpressPCB will produce three 3.8 x 2.5 inch boards for $62.00. A CAD tool is available from their web site.

CADSoft EAGLE is another possibility for printed circuit board layout. Will its output work for the ExpressPCB service?

Connectors

See Digi-Key part numbers SC1052-ND (plug) and SC1153-ND (jack) from this DigiKey catalog page. , or this. These parts match the 2.1mm that I measured below. data sheet for SC1153-ND .

I measured the inside diameter of the plug by inserting the chuck-end of various drill bits to see what would fit. A #44 bit is too big but a #45 bit can be inserted into the center hole of the plug.

#44 too big 0.085 inch 2.15900 mm
#45 fits 0.082 inch 2.0828 mm

Switch

We intend to mount the switch in the center hole of a K'nex connector. That hole is 0.245 inch deep and 0.25 inch in diameter.

DPDT toggle switch, on-off-on, quarter inch threaded bushing vertical PC mount.

digikey search query: DPDT toggle switch pc on-off-on and then filter further by PCB

Layout Issues

Is the body of the connector jacks taller than the body of the switch? The connectors are 11.0mm tall. The switch is only 8.89mm tall. Therefore the connectors must be mounted on the opposite side of the pc board from the switch.
