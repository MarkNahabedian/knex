Knex Measurement Chart

For ease of calculating measurements by avoiding decimal arithmetic on irrational numbers, we can consider all distances in the KNEX world as having two integer components. The first component is a multiple of a fundamental unit. The second is a multiple of that fundamental unit length multiplied by the square root of two. WHen noting measurements, we can show the number of units followed by the number of square root of two units separated by a '#' character, so for example, a right triangle formed by two green legs of length 0#1 each and a white hypotanuse of length 2#0 would have a perimeter or 2#2.
